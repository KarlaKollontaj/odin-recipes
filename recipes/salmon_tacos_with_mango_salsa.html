Salmon tacos with mango salsa

Description

Grilled salmon wrapped up in a small tortilla, topped with peach-mango salsa, shredded cabbage, avocado, and a special sauce.

Ingredients

Mango salsa

  • 3 mangoes, diced
  • 2 peaches, diced
  • 3 poblano peppers, seeded and diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/2 lime, juiced
  • 1 bunch cilantro, chopped, divided

Special Sauce

  • 1 container sour cream
  • 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 26corn tortillas
  • cooking spray
  • 2 pounds fresh salmon
  • 1 small head cabbage, shredded
  • 3 avocados, sliced
  • 2 limes, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Step 1: Combine mangoes, peaches, poblano peppers, red onion, juice of 1/2 a lime, and 1/2 the cilantro in a large bowl to make mango salsa. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate, at least 1 hour, preferably overnight.
  2. Step 2: Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, ketchup, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl to make special sauce.
  3. Step 3: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Arrange tortillas on a baking sheet.
  4. Step 4: Preheat a grill pan over medium-high heat; coat with cooking spray. Cook salmon until easily flaked with a fork, about 4 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate and flake into smaller pieces with a fork.
  5. Step 5: Heat tortillas in the preheated oven until warmed through, about 5 minutes.
  6. Step 6: Divide salmon among tortillas. Top with mango salsa, special sauce, shredded cabbage, avocados, and remaining cilantro. Wrap up tacos and serve lime wedges alongside.
